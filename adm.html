WHY QUANTUM ?


Concept Building Complete Study Material
Batch wise classes are formed with a limited number of students. Lectures are designed according to board pattern covering the depth of JEE Main/Advanced syllabus for Rankers Batch. In Master and Foundation batch lectures are delivered separately for NTSE / Olympiads and School exams covering all the topics effectively with practice tests. Revision classes are arranged regularly for the benefit of students. Doubt Clearing Sessions are regularly scheduled in Time Table and also separately arranged for those students who require extra assistance for clarification of their doubts. These sessions are optional. Our team developed the finest study material of different competitive, School and Board exams for class classes 5th to 12th. Our study material comprises of more than 10,000 pages and 40,000 Questions with solutions. These specially designed study material develop writing skill and understanding required for Board and School exams of the respective classes. Multi exercise booklet, designed according to the syllabus of JEE, containing different types of conceptual, tricky and brain storming questions, including previous ten years JEE Main/Advanced questions, covering all the possible problems of the area which may be asked in the forthcoming exams.
Timely Completion of Course Revision Program
Timely completion of course and topics are necessary to provide an ample amount of time for the students to prepare well. Revision program involves a quick & sharp review of the important topics which covers the entire syllabus at a glance. Special test are also conducted on chapterwise topic as a part of revision program.
Class Room Questions Test Series Programme
Prior to entering into the class, a comprehensive list of questions to be dealt in the lecture is given to the students, as this helps to make-up their minds & creates awareness regarding the topic. This reflects the level of sincerity and commitment which Mitesh Rathi Classes pledges. Quantum Classes has designed test series program for the benefit of students. The entire test series program is for helping students to prepare for JEE Main/JEE Advanced/Board exams/Olympiads/NTSE/School exams. Mock JEE Test Series is based on advanced pattern and conducted for Rankers Batch.
